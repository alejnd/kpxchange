Security

s

Some useful information about usage security.

We don't track you, log you or collect any private data. Https connections don't show your complete URL path, only the domain, so your ID is safe against most common sniffing tecniques. Be careful if you use it from a browser, trackers and history can expose your ID, that's the reason why this service do not offer a web interface to manage your files (and because im lazy). So if you use it carefully and knowing the limitations it's faily safe.
